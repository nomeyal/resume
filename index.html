About

Hi!
I'm Noam, 31 years old, married to my beautiful wife, Maya. I love to laugh and make others laugh! Some of my hobbies include pets (I have a lovely dog named Tooti and a cat named Benji), BJJ, piano, and gaming.

I'm an autodidact who loves to learn and develop new skills. I have strong team-oriented communication skills and am independent, results-oriented, and disciplined.

I'm currently working in the animation industry. I have experience in the gaming, explainer, and TV production fields, creating and composing high-quality videos for gaming companies such as Zynga, Sci-Play, SuperPlay, Playtika, and more. I have also worked on TV shows for BabyTV and Kan Hinuchit. I am very hardworking and well organized in my work.

It's time to change my career path.
While exploring career paths aligned with my skills and personal strengths, I determined that a Quality Assurance role suits me well. I bring strong attention to detail and the ability to evaluate products comprehensively, considering multiple dimensions and use cases.

My current objective: studying Manual QA and Automation to secure an entry-level position in Quality Assurance as a QA Engineer.
